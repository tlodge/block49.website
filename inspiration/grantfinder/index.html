What is grant finder?

focused funding

Put simply, there's money out there that can be used to benefit your development and your community, but it's not easy to find. Grantfinder is a tool that links pots of money (grants, freebies, subsidies) with your specific community and development.

Why is it needed?

There are hundreds of grants out there and thousands of trusts and charities with pots of money available. Grantfunder matches sources of money to your development, it could be money to help reduce energy costs, or funding for developing or improving local facilities such as community centers, leisure facilities and local parks. You already have is a community. Grantfinder gives you the opportunity to use its voice positively.

How does it work?

Grantfunder tracks government initiatives and charities and assesses whether they are applicable to your development. It maintains a list of funding opportunities that have been successfully exploited by similar communities and it gathers feedback on success.
